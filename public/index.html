About UICSA


    The University Institute of Computer Science and Application (UICSA) Rani Durgawati University, Jabalpur is an autonomous institute within the jurisdiction of R.D. University, The institute came into existence in the year 1993 by a resolution of the executive council of the university when University Grants Commission, New Delhi accepted the proposal of our University and sanctioned the initial establishment cost.. Later in the year 1997, our M.C.A. programme was also approved by All India Council of Technical Education. …
